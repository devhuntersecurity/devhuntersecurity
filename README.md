### 📖 About Me

![Profile Views](https://komarev.com/ghpvc/?username=idguard-dns)

SysAdmin by trade, Threat Hunter by blood. I am a **Threat Intelligence & Cyber Security Specialist** specializing in mastering the art of packet filtering, DNS hardening, and infrastructure automation. I don't just build systems; I harden them against the evolving landscape of digital warfare.

As the founder of **Lintas Patria Nusantara** (est. 2024), a dedicated Cloud Service Provider, the developer of **IDGuard DNS** (https://github.com/idguard-dns), and the founder of **JawaTimur Cyber Security** (est. 2022), I focus on engineering robust cloud infrastructure, optimizing network architectures, and fostering a strong security ecosystem.
